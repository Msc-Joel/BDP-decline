Joel Konopo

About me:
Joel Konopo is an investigative journalist from Botswana with vast experience in print media and currently
an MSc Data Journalism candidate at Columbia Graduate School Journalism in New York. I am
former editor of Botswana Guardian newspaper and co-founder of INK Centre for Investigative
Journalism, a vibrant non-profit investigative journalism newsroom. In 2016 I worked on the
Panama Papers, lifting the lid on how the rich and powerful in Botswana use tax havens to hide
their wealth. I am also a 2019 John S. Knight Journalism Fellow at Stanford University, where I
explored the intersection between AI and journalism.
